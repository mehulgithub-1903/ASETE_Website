About Us

The Department of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering has been building its legacy since 1965 in the prestigious Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology (IIEST), Shibpur. It has grown its popularity as well as its applicability in modern times manifold. We, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers, are the ones who design, make, create and introspect the realms of electronics, signal processing, antennae, radio technology, VLSI, etc. that make computing possible. In the global village we live in, we can't stand without this essential branch of engineering, which bridges the gap between computer science and electrical engineering.
